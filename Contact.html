Hey friends! I go by Mack and I created this website in order to do more of what makes me happy. And that involves 3 parts.

Part 1: Whether it be a quick roadtrip down the 1 to go camping for the night or a trans-atlantic flight to Barcelona to see my favorite artist perform, I'm an avid adventurer. I wanted a place to track all of my travels and experiences in the hopes that they can help others get out and do more!

Part 2: I love art in all forms, but fashion is my favorite. Styling has always been a creative outlet for me and the greatest form of self expression. Being able to create looks that inspire others to step outside their comfort zones never gets old.

Part 3: It all started when I won the technology award in the 7th grade. Thank you Mr. Montes. The nerd in me has since developed a passion for coding and I wanted to build my own website as a personal project/portfolio. Perpetually a WIP.

I hope JockinStyle helps you find something to inspire you to get after it (whatever it may be). Thanks for stopping by and please don't hesitate to reach out and say hello!
